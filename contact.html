Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Sisya Comfort

How can I contribute study materials to Sisya Comfort?

You can contribute by using our upload forms on the Books and Study Material pages. We accept PDFs, documents, and links to valuable resources. All submissions are reviewed before publishing.

Is Sisya Comfort completely free for students?

Yes, Sisya Comfort is completely free for all students. We believe in making quality education accessible to everyone without financial barriers.

How often is new content added to the platform?

We add new content weekly. Our team regularly updates study materials, books, and resources to ensure they align with current syllabi and exam patterns.

Can I request specific study materials?

Absolutely! You can request specific materials by contacting us through this form or emailing us directly at requests@sisyacomfort.com. We prioritize requests based on demand.
